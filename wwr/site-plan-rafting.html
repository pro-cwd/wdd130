Overview

Purpose

Jungle rivers in the Amazon part of the Andes are generally larger and have more consistent flows than those in the western Andes. They are also the cleanest and most picturesque rivers in all of Ecuador. In addition to its exceptional whitewater, the rivers of eastern Ecuador also offer the opportunity to experience the incredible Amazon rainforest and the rhythm of life of its native inhabitants.

Audience

Who can go rafting? This question has many variables. However, the answer is everyone who wants to do it. Since there are 6 levels of difficulty of rapid water, the passenger can choose the one that best suits the level of adrenaline he wants to experience. To know more about the different classes of fast waters please review the Classification of Rivers. On the other hand, the minimum age for rafting is 4 years (Rivers class II like the Anzu or Napo river) and the maximum age depends on the physical condition of the passenger.
